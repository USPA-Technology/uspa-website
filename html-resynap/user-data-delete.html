User Data Deletion Request

Your right to control your data is important to us.

How to Request Deletion of Your Data

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data that we have collected through your interactions with our website (e.g., through contact forms, newsletter sign-ups, etc.). Please note that this process applies only to data stored by ReSynap's official services and not to any data you process using the ReSynap open-source framework within your own systems.

To submit a request, please follow these steps:

  1. Compose an Email: Write an email from the address associated with your data. If you don't have an account but have submitted data via a form, please use the email address you provided.
  2. Subject Line: Use the subject line "Data Deletion Request" to help us process your request efficiently.
  3. Identify Yourself: In the body of the email, please include your full name and any other relevant information that can help us identify your data, such as your username or the date you contacted us.
  4. Send the Email: Send your request to our privacy team at privacy@resynap.com.

What to Expect

Once we receive your request, we will send you a confirmation email. We will then proceed to locate and delete your personal data from our systems. This process may take up to 30 days. We will notify you again once the deletion is complete. Please be aware that we may be required to retain certain information for legal or security purposes. We will inform you if this is the case.
