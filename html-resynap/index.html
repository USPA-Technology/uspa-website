Core Features

Text Editor Mind Mapping

Create and customize your mind maps effortlessly using our intuitive text editor. Simply type your ideas, and ReSynap will transform them into a visual representation of your knowledge.

Content & Video Navigation

Enrich your mind maps with multimedia content. Embed videos, links, and other resources directly into your nodes to create a dynamic and interactive learning experience.

Text Chat to Mind Map

Transform your text conversations into insightful mind maps. ReSynap can analyze your chat logs and automatically generate a visual representation of the key ideas and relationships.
